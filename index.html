About us

Joint-stock company "ROSSKAT" (Neftegorsk, Togliatti) - was founded in 1991. Manufacturer with a full production cycle from the production of copper-containing raw materials to the manufacture of cable and wire products for various purposes.

The quality of products is largely ensured by modern equipment of such brands as: Niehoff, Troester and Maerz (Germany), Rosendahl (Austria), SouthWire (USA), Technocable and Caballe (Spain), AGIE (Switzerland), Lloyd Instruments Ltd (England) and other JSC "ROSSKAT" is certified in accordance with the quality management system ISO 9001: 2015 (TUV certificate of conformity) and in accordance with the requirements of GOST ISO 14001-2016 (ISO 14001: 2015)

Purchase of copper-containing raw materials and copper scrap

ROSSKAT Joint-Stock Company adheres to the policy of using transparent

procedures for selecting a supplier. The main objective of the procurement activities of ROSSKAT JSC is to provide companies with goods, works and services in a timely manner with the necessary level of security and the optimal combination of price-quality criteria. Based on a competitive and fair selection of suppliers and contractors.


The company is interested in building legitimate, sustainable and mutually beneficial partnerships with all interested parties based on compliance with laws and regulations, industry standards, contractual and other obligations.
